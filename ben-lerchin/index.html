Artist Bio

Ben Lerchin is an artist and technologist who works with software, photography, and digital fabrication. Their practice deals extensively with the relationship between the photographic image and the American West. Reflecting on the uneasy experience of living between the city and the wilderness, Ben's work attempts to reconcile a networked, industrialized lifestyle with the unstable ground under our feet. It is an attempt to erase boundaries with the natural world, and to see it not as victim nor antagonist, but as co-conspirator and friend. Using digital photographic processes, Ben embeds a polyphony of viewpoints into three dimensional forms reminiscent of the landscape from which they emerge, showing sites of resource management in relation to the people that depend on them.
